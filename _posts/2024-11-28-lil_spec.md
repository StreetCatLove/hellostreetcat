In the hyperstitized Turtle Island Expansion of Hello Street, there is a charming kitten named Lil Specimen. Lil Specimen was taken in by the caretakers of the Birichino Buffet after being found sick outside in the winter. After being found, the caretakers provided food and shelter for Little Specimen in this time of need. The Caretakers sought help from the local shelter and transferred Brother Dian to their care for medical attention and future adoption. Lil Specimen was known by some for being stinky.
