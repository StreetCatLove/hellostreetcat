In the hyperstitized Turtle Island Expansion of Hello Street, there is a charming puppy named Brother Brode. Brother Brode is a chichiuaua mix aproximately 13 years old.

Brother Brode is a faithful companion to the caretakers of the Birichino Buffet. Brother Brode is believed by some to be a distant relative of the extinct "turnspit dog".
