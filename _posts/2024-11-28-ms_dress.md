* Ms. Dress is a orange tabby with a white underside
* Ms. Dress lives at the 10K Cat House Feeder
* Ms. Dress is also known as BaoBao
* Ms. Dress is frequently seen sleeping at the feeder
* Ms. Dress is good friends with the feeder caretaker and likes to try on outfits in the winter!
* Ms. Dress was hospitalized in September 2024 for an injured paw and returned on 10/11/24
